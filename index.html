About Me

As a grad student at Carnegie Mellon University, I'm pursuing my MS in Engineering and Technology Innovation Management, focusing on applying machine learning and data science to solve real-world problems. Passionate about exploring digital twin technology, I aim to create virtual replicas of physical assets for data-driven optimization

Before CMU, I served as Head of Operations for the Primary Division at ITC Limited, India's largest tobacco company. Leading a team of 100+, I ensured timely delivery of high-quality cut tobacco to Secondary and OCMs. Spearheading digital transformation initiatives, I led projects in data sciences and Industry 4.0, implementing solutions like predictive maintenance and IIoT across production, engineering, and supply chain domains. With a strong mechanical engineering background from IIT Delhi, I gained experience as a product intern at JCB. Additionally, I hold certifications in data analytics, web development, and Go concurrency from institutions like DeepLearning.AI and Johns Hopkins University
